Citations and good-law flags are resolved from a curated index of landmark judgments and should always be confirmed via the Verify on Indian Kanoon link. “Needs lookup” means a case isn’t in the index — not that it’s wrong. How it works

Paste any text. The tool finds every case — by name, by citation, or by “X v Y” in any form — resolves landmark judgments to their authoritative citation, flags overruled / doubted cases, and detects statutes & Articles. Each item gets a verify link. Your runs stay in History on this browser only; nothing is uploaded.
